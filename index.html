What We Offer

Our Services

Lawn Mowing

Clean, precise cuts every visit. We mow, edge, and clean up clippings so your yard always looks its best — one-time or on a regular schedule.

Trimming & Edging

Sharp borders and tidy edges that give your property a polished, well-maintained appearance neighbours will notice.

Snow Removal

Safe, prompt snow clearing for driveways, walkways, and steps. We keep your property accessible no matter what the Calgary winter throws at us.

Spring Cleanup

Start the season fresh. We clear out dead leaves, matted grass, winter debris, and anything left behind by the snow so your lawn can breathe and grow.

Full Yard Cleanup

A complete yard tidy-up — garbage, debris, branches, and general waste removed from your entire property. Great for spring, fall, or after a big storm.

Fall Leaf Cleanup

Full leaf removal from your lawn, garden beds, and walkways before the snow arrives. We rake, bag, and haul everything away so your yard is ready for winter.
